What We Do

Programs and camps to suit you

We offer programs and camps for various skill levels and age groups, throughout the spring and summer months to keep your child active, and happy.

Well-rounded skill development

We focus on all aspects of the game, to give your child a well-rounded approach to their skill development.

Reliable teaching

Absolute Hockey School is run by passionate instructors who have had years of combined hockey training and instructing experience.

Programs

What programs are available?

We offer private instruction, team instruction, small group instruction, and seasonal sessions.

Where are the programs located?

All programs will take place at the East Gwillimbury Sports Complex.

What is the cost?

When attending a group of sessions, the cost is $30/session. Drop in rates are available at $35/session (with a minimum of 2 hours notice), and family rates are available upon request.

Camps

What is included in the camps?

Summer camps include 3 hours of on-ice instruction and one 1 hour of off-ice dryland (tailored specifically for the age group).

What skills are focused on?

We focus on drills centered around lateral mobility, stops and starts, crossovers, efficient skating strides, stick handling, passing, puck protection, shooting, and scoring.

What does my child need to bring?

Each child needs to bring certified hockey equipment, lunch and snacks (that contain no nuts or related allergens), shorts, running shoes, and a t-shirt. Bottled water will be provided at no cost.

What is the cost?

The cost is $350 (including tax), subject to space availability. An early bird discount of $50 will be applied when registering before May 1, 2017.

Where is the camp located?

The camp with take place at the Aurora Family Leisure Complex.

Summer Camp

Our Philosophy

Absolute Hockey School is committed to their students reaching their full potential from the Basic Hockey Skills Program to the Advanced Hockey Skills Program. The philosophy of Absolute Hockey School is to increase a child's hockey skills in a fun and affordable way. As well as affordability, Absolute Hockey School will provide excellent teaching by highly qualified professionals to improve a child's skating and technical skills, and ultimately their game.
