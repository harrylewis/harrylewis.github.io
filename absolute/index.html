A variety of programs and camps

Skill-based approach

Effective teaching

Absolute Hockey School is run by passionate instructors who have had years of combined hockey training and instructing experience.

Programs

What programs are available?

We offer private instruction, team instruction, small group instruction, and seasonal sessions.

Where are the programs located?

All programs will take place at a rink located either in Newmarket, Aurora, or East Gwillimbury.

What is the cost?

When attending a group of sessions, the cost is $30/session. Drop in rates are available at $35/session (with a minimum of 2 hours notice).

The total cost is $60 for both, and is subject to the family rate where applicable. Please call or email for more information.

Camps

What is included in the camps?

Summer camps include 3 per day hours of on-ice instruction and one 1 per day hour of off-ice dryland (tailored specifically for the age group).

What skills are focused on?

We focus on drills centered around lateral mobility, stops and starts, crossovers, efficient skating strides, stick handling, passing, puck protection, shooting, and scoring.

What does my child need to bring?

Each child needs to bring certified hockey equipment, lunch and snacks (that contain no nuts or related allergens), shorts, running shoes, and a t-shirt. Bottled water will be provided at no cost.

What is the cost?

The cost is $350 (including tax), subject to space availability. An early bird discount of $50 will be applied when registering before May 1, 2017.

Where is the camp located?

All programs will take place at a rink located either in Newmarket, Aurora, or East Gwillimbury.

Summer Camp

The Summer Camp will take place from August 14, 2017 to August 18, 2017 at the Aurora Family Leisure Complex. Drop off is at 8:30 AM - 9:00 AM, and pick up is at 3:45 PM - 4:00 PM.

Our Philosophy

Absolute Hockey School is committed to their athletes reaching their full potential. By focusing on the fundamental skills necessary to be successful at hockey, we are able to develop a strong foundation in our athletes, so that they can continue to see progress and improve. We ensure that our instructors are highly qualified so that they can help to foster your athlete’s skating abilities and technical skills, and ultimately their game.
